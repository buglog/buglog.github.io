2012

2012 is a six minute animated short directed by Ingo Raschka. It was made using an archaic technique called cel animation. Once the most commonly used animation process worldwide, cel animation was succeeded by digital paint techniques around the turn of the century.

2012 is an uncomfortable first date story. It's inspired by Los Angeles, the Maya, climate change, and my friends.

This film would not have been able to be made without the tireless work of Sam Maurer, Noah Malone, Alvin Fai, Mabel Ye, Zach Marcus, Chris and Lydie Raschka, Hedda Durnbaugh, Yonatan Tal, and Josh Swallow.
